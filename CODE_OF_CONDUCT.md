# Contributor Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

In the interest of fostering an open and welcoming environment, we as contributors, maintainers, and participants pledge to make participation in our project and our community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, level of
socioeconomic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

We pledge to treat all participants with respect.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment include:

*   Using welcoming and inclusive language [2].
*   Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences [2].
*   Gracefully accepting constructive feedback.
*   Prioritizing the community's well-being over personal gain [2].

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

*   Harassment, including but not limited to offensive comments, intimidation, and other forms of bullying [2, 8].
*   Trolling, insulting or derogatory comments, and personal or political attacks [8].
*   Public or private harassment [8].
*   Publishing others' private information, such as a physical or electronic address, without explicit permission.
*   Other conduct which could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional or community setting [2, 8].

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to our project's spaces, and also when an individual is officially representing the project or its community in public spaces [6]. Representation of the project may include using an official e-mail address, posting via an official social media account, or acting as an appointed representative at an online or offline event [6].

## Enforcement Responsibilities

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and upholding acceptable behaviors and will take appropriate and fair action in response to any unacceptable behavior [2].

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported to the project maintainers by contacting [contact email or method]. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated to determine an appropriate response [2].

All reports will be handled with discretion and confidentiality [2, 3]. Maintainers are expected to respect the privacy and security of the reporter at all times [2, 3].

Consequences of unacceptable behavior may include:

*   A warning with a request for cessation of the behavior.
*   A temporary ban from some or all public interaction with the community for a specified period [8].
*   A permanent ban from all project spaces [8].
